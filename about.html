Our Mission

At National Environmental Management Authority "NEMA", we are dedicated to the sustainable management and conservation of natural resources. Our mission is to ensure that our planet's precious resources are used responsibly and preserved for future generations.

Card title

Our Story Founded in 2015, National Envronmental Management Athourity began as a small group of environmental enthusiasts passionate about protecting nature. Over the years, we have grown into a leading organization advocating for the conservation of forests, water bodies, minerals, and wildlife. Our journey started with a single project aimed at reforestation, and today, we have expanded our efforts globally, impacting communities and ecosystems worldwide..

Our Vision and Goals and Our Values

Our Vision and Goals
Our vision is a world where natural resources are managed sustainably, ensuring a balanced and healthy ecosystem. We aim to: Promote sustainable practices in resource management. Educate and empower communities to protect their natural surroundings. Advocate for policies that support environmental conservation.

Our Values

Sustainability: We believe in meeting the needs of the present without compromising the future. Integrity: We conduct our work with honesty and transparency. Collaboration: We work together with communities, governments, and other organizations to achieve our goals. Innovation: We strive to find new and effective ways to conserve natural resources..
