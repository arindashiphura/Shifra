NATURAL RESOURCES IS DIVIDED INTO TWO
RENEWABLE RESOURCES AND NON RENEWABLE RESOURCES

Natural resources are materials from the Earth that are used to support life and meet people's
needs. Any natural substance that humans use can be considered a natural resource. Oil, coal,
natural gas, metals, stone and sand are natural resources. Other natural resources are air,
sunlight, soil and water.
