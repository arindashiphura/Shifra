About Natural Resources

Natural resources are materials or substances that occur in nature and can be used for economic gain. They include:

  • Water: Essential for all forms of life, water is a crucial natural resource.
  • Minerals: Used in various industries, minerals like gold, silver, and iron are valuable resources.
  • Forests: Provide timber, oxygen, and habitat for wildlife.
  • Fossil Fuels: Includes coal, oil, and natural gas, which are major energy sources.
  • Soil: Important for agriculture and plant growth.
